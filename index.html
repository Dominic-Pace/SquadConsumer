What is Squad?

Running a business is hard enough without spending time making schedules. That’s why we’re introducing Squad - a machine-learning application for smart operations management. Squad learns how you like your team's schedule and makes it for you. Approve these recommendations, make minor changes or start with a custom template - it’s totally up to you.

We’re not sure what’s worse - being understaffed or overstaffed. With Squad, you won’t have to worry about either. Squad integrates multiple data points to determine the right amount of coverage for every situation. A happy employee makes for a productive employee, and Squad’s user-friendly interface engages employees with the rest of their team. You know the old way you’ve been doing scheduling. It's time to let Squad take care of scheduling for you.
